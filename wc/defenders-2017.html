Defender leaderboard — 2017 World Championships

Every partnership with ≥40 defended boards in each division, ranked by the IMPs their defence gained versus the field in the identical contract on the same deal. Both partners share every board; the two columns on the right split that IMP result by which partner made the opening lead.

Source Lyon 2017 · RR + KO · peers = other tables in the same contract on that deal · leave-one-out · caveat: results can't fully separate great defence from declarer error — read vs the field

F1 · tricks vs DD

Double-dummy declarer tricks − actual. Positive = you held declarer below double-dummy.

F2 · tricks vs field

Held declarer to fewer tricks than other defenders in the same contract on the deal. Pure defence in tricks.

F3 · defeating vs field

Did you defeat it − the field's set-rate for that contract. Positive = you set ones the field lets through.

F4 · IMPs vs field · on lead

The ranking. F2's trick edge in IMPs from the defending seat. The two right-hand columns show F4 only on boards where that partner led.
